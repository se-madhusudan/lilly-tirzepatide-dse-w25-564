As little as 3% weight loss is associated with metabolic adaptation, which results in a tendency toward weight regain.19,20,22,23

Studies have also shown that some antihyperglycemic agents may be associated with weight gain.14,24

Having conversations about weight to support T2D management can be another barrier for both HCPs and patients. But these conversations are important because weight management may support glycemic control and facilitate more comprehensive diabetes care.
